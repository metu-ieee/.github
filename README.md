# Middle East Technical University IEEE Student Branch

Welcome to the official open-source organization of METU IEEE Student Chapter! This organization houses the project codabases, education materials and society's tools developed to satisfy the society's needs.

This organization has been built to become one of the society's everlasting legacies, assure technical perfection of the society, and provide a realistic experience of being a real engineer to the members under IEEE.

---

## Current Societies Working in This Organization:

### IEEE Computer Society (CS)
As IEEE CS, we are not solely Computer Engineers nor Electrical & Electronics Engineers; we are a huge family, made of every passionate member who wants to build, develop and contribute. However, the story is not that short; when you end up at IEEE CS, you need to experience the Computer Science as you should: Building systems, working on projects, thinking just like an expert and executing like a professional. It is not easy, but is has also never been easy. Therefore IEEE CS is always with and for you. To make you achieve what you imagine to be.

You will see our current focuses and past work here: Oh wait! We are working on it! Just give us some time, this place will be full in a year or so!
